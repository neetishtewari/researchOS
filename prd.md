PRD — ResearchOS

AI Operating System for Market Research Teams

1. Product Vision

ResearchOS is a private AI workspace that augments how professional market research teams:

Win clients

Design research

Generate insights

Deliver high-quality recommendations

ResearchOS removes low-value effort while preserving expert judgment at every step.

2. Problem Statement

Market research firms face:

High senior dependency for proposals and research design

Slow turnaround between fieldwork and insights

Inconsistent storytelling quality

Underutilized historical knowledge locked in documents

Existing tools optimize execution, not thinking.

ResearchOS fills this gap.

3. Target Users
Primary

Partners / Directors

Research Managers

Senior Analysts

Secondary

Junior researchers (guided usage)

4. Core Value Proposition

“ResearchOS acts as a thinking multiplier across the research lifecycle—without replacing human expertise.”

5. Product Principles

Human-in-the-loop by default

Explainable outputs, not black boxes

Consulting-grade language and structure

Assist decisions, never make them

Incremental adoption, not forced change

6. Scope (v1)
Included

Web-based private workspace

Four AI agents

Context upload and retrieval

Structured outputs

Explicit assumptions & reasoning

Excluded (by design)

Full workflow automation

CRM or survey tool integrations

Client-facing dashboards

Auto-sending or publishing

7. Core Modules (v1)
7.1 ResearchOS — Proposals

Users: Partners, BD

Purpose:
Accelerate proposal creation while preserving quality.

Inputs:

RFPs

Client emails

Internal notes

Outputs:

Problem understanding

Research objectives

Recommended approach

Methodology

Timeline & deliverables

Assumptions & open questions

Success Metric:
Proposal draft time ↓ 50%+

7.2 ResearchOS — Leads

Users: Leadership, BD

Purpose:
Improve lead qualification and prioritization.

Inputs:

Inbound emails

LinkedIn messages

RFPs

Outputs:

Lead type & complexity

Effort vs value assessment

Risks & unknowns

Recommended next steps

Success Metric:
Faster response, higher win quality

7.3 ResearchOS — Design

Users: Research Managers

Purpose:
Translate business problems into strong research designs.

Inputs:

Business objectives

Constraints (time, budget, geography)

Outputs:

Research questions

Methodology rationale

Sample logic

Draft questionnaire / discussion guide

Risks & limitations

Success Metric:
Kickoff time ↓ 30%

7.4 ResearchOS — Synthesis

Users: Analysts, Consultants

Purpose:
Turn raw data into actionable insights.

Inputs:

Transcripts

Open-ended responses

Analyst notes

Outputs:

Key themes

Insight statements

Supporting evidence

Business implications

Validation questions

Success Metric:
Analysis effort ↓ 40%
